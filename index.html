Know the warning signs

6 Signs Your Panel
Needs Replacing

If your home was built before 2000, watch for these red flags.

01

Breakers That Won't Stay Reset

A breaker that trips repeatedly means your panel can't safely handle your home's electrical load — it's working overtime to prevent a fire.

⚠ High Risk
02

The Panel Is Warm to the Touch

Heat radiating from your panel indicates loose connections, overloaded circuits, or internal arcing — all potential fire starters.

⚠ Critical
03

You Have a Recalled Brand

Federal Pacific, Zinsco, and Pushmatic panels have known safety defects. If you have one, it needs immediate replacement.

⚠ Urgent
04

Only 100-Amp Service

Today's homes need 200 amps minimum for EVs, solar, and whole-home AC. An undersized panel can become dangerously overloaded.

⚠ Capacity Issue
05

Fuse Box Instead of Breakers

If your home uses screw-in fuses, you have one of the oldest electrical systems possible — a major safety and insurance liability.

⚠ Replace Now
06

Lights Dim When Appliances Run

Voltage sags when your dryer kicks on? Your panel is struggling to distribute power. This worsens over time.

⚠ Performance
